About

Hello! I'm Ville Joe Catig, a passionate Freelance Full-Stack Web Developer, I love solving problems, learning new tools, and building websites that are clean, responsive, and functional.

Studied at Kolehiyo ng Lungsod ng Dasmariñas, where I built a solid foundation in programming and problem-solving.

I don't have formal industry experience yet, I've worked on personal and freelance projects that helped me build real-world skills in both frontend and backend development.

While I'm still growing in my journey, others have noticed my strengths in programming. I genuinely enjoy what I do and continuously look for opportunities to learn and grow.
